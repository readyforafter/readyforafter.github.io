Meet The Fam

Divine Bradley

Divine, a committed Father and serviceman to all people. He believes that the end is just the beginning and he wants to remembered as a symbol of hope and advocacy for the power of imagination.

Joseph Weissgold

Joseph wants to be remembered not for what he did but how we went about doing it. His plan is to select a place with a beautiful view where people can ‘visit’ him after he’s gone.

Michelle Arrazcaeta

Michelle is a Daughter, Step Sister & Auntie, Cousin and Friend that wants her funeral to be a reverse birthday party with gifts of appreciation for fam and friends, music, dancing and cry laughing.

Sallomé

Sallomé wants to be remembered as an abolitionist who helped to bring about the end of forms of indentured servitude in the United States.

Sergio Venegas

Sergio wants to be remembered for adding colors (of joy) to the lives of his family, his partner, and friends. He wants his body to be cremated, his ashes to be mixed with sparkles and spread in simultaneous joyful celebrations in his hometown in Costa Rica, NYC, and Mexico.

Andrew Mangino

Andrew wants to be remembered for helping all people create their legacy on their terms while enjoying the unexpected wonders of life. His funeral will be a 24-hour film score conduct-a-thon in which each hour honors a historic decade and location. Costumes and accents encouraged.
